This Chrome Extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

EA Ping Monitor simply displays a real-time ping value to EA's public FUT servers inside the official EA Sports FC Web App. No user information, account details, or gameplay data is ever accessed, processed, or transmitted by this extension.

The extension runs locally in your browser and is fully self-contained. No data is sent to any third-party servers.
